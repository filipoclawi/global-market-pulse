04 / METHODOLOGY

What the signal means

Equal weighted. Every available market contributes equally, preventing the largest US benchmarks from dominating the global pulse.

Comparable. Percentage changes are dimensionless, so currencies and index-point scales do not need conversion.

Transparent proxies. A visible “Proxy” tag marks index-tracking ETFs used where a dependable free benchmark history is unavailable.

Market-day aware. Date comparisons use each market’s closest close on or before the chosen date. Coverage is always displayed.

Data sources and limitations

Daily market data is fetched from Yahoo Finance. Values may be delayed. ETF proxies use adjusted closes to remove artificial stock-split jumps and can differ from their benchmarks because of distributions, fees, trading hours and tracking error.
